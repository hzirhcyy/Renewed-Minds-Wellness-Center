Learn more about mental health and discover ways to take care of your emotional well-being.

Anxiety

Anxiety is a feeling of worry, fear, or nervousness that can affect a person's daily life.

Common Symptoms:

  • Excessive worrying
  • Restlessness
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Rapid heartbeat

Depression

Depression is a mental health condition that causes persistent feelings of sadness and loss of interest.

Common Symptoms:

  • Persistent sadness
  • Low energy
  • Hopelessness
  • Sleep problems

Stress

Stress is the body's response to challenges or demands that can affect physical and emotional health.

Common Symptoms:

  • Headaches
  • Fatigue
  • Irritability
  • Difficulty sleeping

Burnout

Burnout is a state of physical and emotional exhaustion caused by prolonged stress.

Common Symptoms:

  • Feeling overwhelmed
  • Lack of motivation
  • Exhaustion
  • Reduced productivity

Bipolar

Bipolar disorder is a mental health condition that causes extreme mood swings, alternating between emotional highs (mania) and lows (depression).

Common Symptoms:

  • Physical Changes
  • Loss of Interest
  • Feelings of Worthlessness
  • Suicidal Thoughts

Schizophrenia

Schizophrenia is a serious, chronic mental health disorder that affects how a person thinks, feels and behaves. It is characterized by a distorted perception of reality.

Common Symptoms:

  • Hallucinations
  • Delusions
  • Lack of Motivation
  • Memory Issues
