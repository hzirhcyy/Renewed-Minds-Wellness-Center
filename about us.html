About Us

Empowering individuals to prioritise mental wellness.

Company History

Renewed Minds Wellness Center was founded in 2022 by a group of mental health advocates, psychologists and volunteers who recognized the growing need for mental health education and emotional support.

The organization was created to bridge the gap between professional mental health services and public awareness by offering educational programs, counselling support and community engagement activities.

Mission

  • To promote mental health awareness through education and community engagement.
  • To provide accessible, compassionate and professional mental health support services.
  • To reduce the stigma surrounding mental health by encouraging open conversations and acceptance.

Vision

To create a society where mental health is valued, understood and accessible to everyone, empowering individuals to live healthier, happier and more resilient lives.

Objectives

  • Increase public awareness of mental health issues through campaigns and educational programs.
  • Provide high-quality counselling and mental wellness services for individuals of all ages.

Need Immediate Support?

You are not alone. Reach out to a trusted person or seek professional help if you are struggling with your mental health.
